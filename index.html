FAQ

What is a .build file?+
A .build file is a packaged project file exclusive to OXYX STORE. It contains your complete project in a single distributable format that's easy to share and deploy.
How do I upload my build?+
Go to UPLOAD BUILD in the navbar. Upload your .build file, add a preview photo, fill in the details and submit. The owner reviews within 24 hours before it goes live.
Are free builds actually free?+
Yes, 100% free. Click the build, hit Download, and get the .build file immediately — no payment required beyond your OXYX account.
How do I buy a premium build?+
Click any premium build then BUY NOW. You'll see the seller's contact (WhatsApp or Discord). Reach out directly to arrange payment and receive your file.
Can I sell my own builds?+
Absolutely! Upload your build, set it as Premium, enter your price in IDR, and add your contact. After owner approval, buyers contact you directly. OXYX takes 20% for premium builds.
How long does review take?+
Typically within 24 hours. Track your pending builds in the Upload section. You'll get a notification once approved.
What formats can I upload?+
Only .build files are accepted for the build file, and JPG/PNG/WEBP (max 5MB) for the preview photo. Make sure your project is properly packaged before uploading.
